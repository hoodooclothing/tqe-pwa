How Modes Work
itsscale Instant

Doubles timestamps so TikTok's encoder allocates more bitrate. No re-encoding — lossless and instant. Best for H.264 files.

Re-encode Slow

Full CRF re-encode with libx264. Maximum quality control. Use for non-H.264 files or when you need precise bitrate targeting.

Remux Fast

Swaps the container without re-encoding. Fixes compatibility issues with MKV, WebM, or AVI files.

Auto Smart

Automatically picks itsscale for H.264 files and re-encode for everything else. Recommended for most users.

Avoid These
× Uploading through the TikTok app (adds extra compression on top)
× Exporting with quality slider on default (always max it out)
× Exporting at 4K (TikTok downscales to 1080p anyway, wastes file size)
× Screen recording your edit instead of exporting properly
× Sending your video through WhatsApp or Telegram before uploading
× Re-uploading already compressed TikTok videos (double compression)
× Using "Save as Draft" or "Save to Camera Roll" instead of proper export
